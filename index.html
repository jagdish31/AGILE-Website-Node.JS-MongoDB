About Us

Agile Society, is the technical society of Department of CSE, Parala Maharaja Engineering College, Berhampur in which the teachers and students of the concerned department are members.

  • This society is mainly meant to build up a good academic atmosphere by organising lectures by outside speakers and also by the members of the societies.
  • This also help to promote good relationship between the field departments and industries on one hand and the educational institution on the other.
  • The members also take up modelling work and organise quiz and other competitions regularly.
  • For the academic development, the engineering societies organise many activities.

Mission

To be a center of excellence in technical education by using cutting edge technology
that produce competent engineers of today & tomorrow to serve the society.

Quality

Advancement of the quality of research and academic programs within the organization.

Support

Backing up the skills of the students with a financial support.

Leadership

Promoting leadership qualities by organizing various programs.

Activities

To bring out the best from a student in terms of technical and extracurricular activities.
